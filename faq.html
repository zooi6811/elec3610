Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical delivery time?
Our standard delivery time is 4–6 weeks from order confirmation. For custom designs, please allow up to 8 weeks.
Can I return or exchange my custom piece?
As each piece is made to order, we do not accept returns. However, if there’s a defect or damage, we’ll repair or replace it under warranty.
What materials do you use?
We source responsibly managed and recycled timbers, such as oak, walnut, and sustainably harvested pine. Each material is eco-badged in our design tool.
How do I track my order?
Visit our Order Tracking page, enter your Order ID, and click “Track” — you’ll see real-time status updates from “Order Confirmed” through to delivery.
Can I speak to a real person?
Of course! Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of any page to connect with our support team live.
